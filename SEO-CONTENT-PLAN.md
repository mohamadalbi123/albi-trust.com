# Albitrust SEO Content Plan

## Goal

Grow qualified organic traffic from struggling retail traders and turn that traffic into assessment starts.

Primary conversion target:

`Take the free trading assessment on Albitrust and discover your biggest blocker as a trader.`

## Why This Is The Right SEO Direction

Albitrust should not compete first on broad trading keywords like:

- `forex strategy`
- `best forex indicator`
- `how to trade forex`

Those are crowded, vague, and often attract the wrong audience.

Albitrust is strongest when it speaks to traders who already feel pain:

- they are not profitable
- they keep repeating mistakes
- they feel stuck
- they want clarity about what is wrong

That is where the product and the SEO strategy align naturally.

## Core SEO Strategy

Focus on 3 content layers:

1. Assessment landing pages
2. Problem-based blog articles
3. Cluster pages around recurring trader blockers

The assessment pages should be the conversion center.
The blog should build trust and emotional connection.
The cluster structure should help Google understand topical depth.

## Writing Rules

- Open with pain immediately
- Keep paragraphs short
- Speak to the trader directly
- Focus on behavior, not indicators
- Show the real reason behind the behavior
- Give practical corrections, not generic motivation
- End with the assessment CTA every time

## Best CTA Format

Use this exact CTA at the end of each article:

`Take the free trading assessment on Albitrust and discover your biggest blocker as a trader.`

## Internal Linking Rules

Every article should link to:

- the main assessment page
- one related blocker article
- one related discipline or psychology article

Example:

An overtrading article should link to:

- `/trading-assessment`
- `/blog/how-to-stop-revenge-trading`
- `/blog/how-to-improve-trading-discipline`

## SEO On-Page Rules

- Put the primary keyword in the title
- Put the primary keyword in the H1
- Use it naturally in the intro
- Add 3-6 related sub-keywords in H2s
- Write a strong meta description focused on pain
- Keep URLs short and direct

## Example Meta Description Style

`If you are not profitable in trading, the problem may not be your strategy. Learn the real reasons traders stay stuck and how to identify your biggest blocker.`
